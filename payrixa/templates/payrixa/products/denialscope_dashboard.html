DenialScope detects sudden spikes in denial dollars that signal payer policy changes or systemic issues.

It analyzes your denial patterns by payer and reason code to catch expensive problems early.

What "denial_dollars_spike" means

A sharp increase in total dollars denied by a specific payer or for a specific reason code. This is different from denial rate‚Äîit's about total financial impact.

What you'll see here

Total Denials: Count of all denied claims in the last 30 days
Top Payer: Which payer is denying the most claims
Top Reason: Most common denial reason code

Understanding signals

High/Critical: Large dollar spike, investigate immediately
Medium: Notable increase, review this week
Low: Small variance, monitor for trend

When to care

Act now: New spike in top payer or unfamiliar denial code
Monitor: Known issues or seasonal patterns
Note for later: Low-dollar variances
