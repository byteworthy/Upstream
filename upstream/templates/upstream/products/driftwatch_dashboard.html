üìä What is DriftWatch?

DriftWatch detects when payers start denying your claims at different rates than normal.

We compare this week to your normal rate (last 4 weeks). If it's unusual, we flag it.

What "DENIAL_RATE" means

The percentage of claims a payer denies. If they normally deny 8% and suddenly deny 15%, that's a 7-point increase worth investigating.

What "Baseline" means

Baseline = Your average denial rate over the last 4 weeks.
Current = This week's denial rate.
Delta = The difference (+ means worse, - means better).

What "Severity" means

High (0.7+): Investigate today
Medium (0.4-0.7): Review this week
Low (<0.4): Monitor for trend

When to care

Investigate today: High severity + large delta (>10 points)
Check this week: Medium severity or recurring patterns
Note for later: Low severity + small delta (<5 points)
